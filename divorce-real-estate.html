Handled With Care

When a marriage ends, the home is often the largest and most emotional asset to divide. You deserve a specialist who protects your equity, your credit, and your peace of mind — with neutrality and discretion.

When a marriage comes apart, the house is rarely just a line on a balance sheet. It is where the kids took their first steps and where a thousand ordinary evenings happened, and now it has to be dealt with during one of the hardest seasons of your life.

Selling or dividing a home in a divorce is unlike any other transaction I handle, and I do not pretend otherwise. There are two people whose interests both deserve respect, there are attorneys and court calendars that set the pace, and there is a weight in the room that an ordinary closing never carries. Done carelessly, it drains money and goodwill from both sides and drags out the very thing everyone wants behind them. Done with care, it quietly becomes one less thing you have to carry while you sort out everything else.

That middle seat, the calm and genuinely neutral one, is where I sit. As a Certified Divorce Real Estate Specialist, my job is to treat both parties fairly, keep the lines of communication open and honest, work hand in hand with your attorneys, and structure the sale so that what matters most is protected. Before real estate, I spent years as a registered nurse, and that work teaches you things that translate more than you would think: how to stay steady when emotions are running high, how to listen for what people are not saying, and how to keep a clear head when the stakes are real. That is the temperament I bring to every divorce file that crosses my desk.

How I Help

Neutral Representation

I treat both spouses fairly and communicate openly with each party and their attorneys — removing the suspicion that the agent is "taking sides."

Attorney Coordination

I work alongside your legal team and financial advisors so the sale aligns with your decree, court orders, and settlement timelines.

Buyout Analysis

If one spouse wishes to keep the home, I help evaluate whether a buyout is feasible and how to structure it fairly.

Strategic Sale & Net Protection

When selling is the path, I position the home to maximize net proceeds and protect both parties through inspection, appraisal, and closing.

Credit & Equity Protection

Careful structuring and documentation help shield each spouse's credit and equity from unnecessary exposure.

Discretion & Privacy

Sensitive circumstances are handled with the confidentiality and professionalism they deserve, start to finish.
