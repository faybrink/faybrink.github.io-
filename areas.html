Areas & Corridors

From the forested calm of The Woodlands to the energy of Midtown, Fay serves Houston's most sought-after corridors with local depth and strategy.

The Woodlands & Spring

Master-planned luxury tucked into the pines up north, with some of the best schools in the state. It is the spot relocating executives and families ask me about first, and for good reason.

Memorial & Energy Corridor

Old-money prestige, big wooded lots, and a quick hop to the Energy Corridor's major employers. This is one of the cornerstones of Houston luxury, and it has held that title for a long time.

Katy

West Houston's powerhouse, home to Cinco Ranch, Elyson, and Cane Island, anchored by top-ranked Katy ISD. If you work the Energy Corridor, you will not find a better commute anywhere in the metro.

Cypress

Fulshear

Small-town charm running headlong into serious luxury growth. Cross Creek Ranch and Weston Lakes lead the way, and the new-construction incentives out here are about the best in Texas right now.

Heights, Montrose & Midtown

Walkable, full of character, and never boring. Think historic bungalows next to sleek new townhomes, with Houston's best food and culture right outside your door.

North Houston

From Tomball up to Conroe, this is where established value meets fresh growth, with I-45 and the Grand Parkway making the rest of the city easy to reach.
